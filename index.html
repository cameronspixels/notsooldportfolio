A Bit About Me

Hi, I'm a 20 year old student living in a flat Manchester. I'm in the final year of my Web Development degree, and have aspiartions to move into front-end website development.

I've been programming for 5 or 6 years now, starting back in the early stages of Minecraft modding alongside running gameservers. That taught me Java and basic object-oriented methods, as well as some PHP and how to use FTP.

I very much went away from programming as a whole for a long time, longing more for the creativity of design. I then came to university initially studying Digital Media/Marketing, but realised very early on that the web development in the course came much more naturally to me than anything else. Best of all, it let me mix my practical programming skills with my want to make things look nice!

Luckily as I advanced into my final year, I was allowed to switch my degree route (thanks Richard!) to Web Development. I didn't dislike marketing as such - I did reasonably well academically in it, and wouldn't have complained about graduating - but this has allowed me to focus on what I'm interested in most.

What Do I Do?

Looking to work on the front-end, I've worked over the years on progressing both design and development skills.
