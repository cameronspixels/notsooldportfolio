A Bit About Me

Hi, I'm a 20-year-old student living in a flat Manchester. I'm in the final year of my Web Development degree and have aspirations to move into front-end website development.

I've been programming for 5 or 6 years now, starting back in the early stages of Minecraft modding, alongside running game servers. That taught me Java and basic object-oriented methods, as well as some PHP and how to use FTP.

I came to university initially studying Digital Media/Marketing, but realised very early on that the web development in the course came much more naturally to me than anything else. It's given me something to apply my previously learned technical skills to, where games and game servers previously didn't.

Luckily as I advanced into my final year, I was allowed to switch my degree route to Web Development. I didn't dislike marketing as such - I did reasonably well academically in it, and wouldn't have complained about graduating - but this has allowed me to focus on what I'm interested in most.
